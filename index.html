Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Property Exchange?

The Property Exchange is a place to explore real estate investment structures, current offerings, educational resources, and the questions investors should consider before making a decision.

How do I request investment access?

Submit your email in the access form above. The current form is a prototype and will be connected to the appropriate access workflow later.

Which strategies can I explore?

You can browse DST properties, Qualified Opportunity Zone Funds, REITs, 721 Exchange strategies, and Mineral Royalties, with additional categories added over time.

Are the full-cycle examples a promise of future performance?

No. Full-cycle examples are provided for context only. Historical performance does not guarantee future results, and every offering requires its own review.
